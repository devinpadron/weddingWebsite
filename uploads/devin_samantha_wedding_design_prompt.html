D & S

June 1–4, 2027  ·  SPAO, Italy

Wedding Website Design Prompt

01 — Aesthetic Direction

Mood

Old World luxury — Italian villa meets contemporary editorial. The feeling of linen napkins, travertine stone, and olive groves at golden hour.

Tone

Refined and unhurried. Never loud. Every element should feel like it was placed deliberately — like a perfectly set table.

References

Vogue Italia editorial, Rosewood Hotels branding, Aesop product pages, Château Pichon Baron website.
